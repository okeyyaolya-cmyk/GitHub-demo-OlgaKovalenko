Zero-Air Churning

Unlike industrial ice cream, our gelato is churned at a much slower speed. This prevents air from being whipped in, resulting in a dense, silky texture that melts perfectly on the tongue.

"Gelato is the poetry of ice."

Slow-Aged Base

We age our base for 24 hours before churning. This allows the proteins to hydrate, creating a stability and richness that can't be rushed.

Our Evolution

2012
The Humble Cart

We served our first scoop of Roasted Pistachio from a restored 1960s tricycle at the local Saturday Market.

2015
The Flagship Opens

Our first brick-and-mortar home opened its doors, featuring a glass-walled 'scoop laboratory' for guests to watch the process.

2019
Sustainability Milestone

Achieved 100% plastic-free packaging and implemented a 'closed-loop' waste program with local community gardens.

2024
National Artisans

Voted 'Best Gelato in the Country' by Culinary Fluidity Magazine, but still serving the same neighborhoods we love.
